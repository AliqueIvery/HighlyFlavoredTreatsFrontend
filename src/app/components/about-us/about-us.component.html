Our Story

Highly Flavored Treats began in my mother’s kitchen in Miami, where my love for baking first took shape. Inspired by the time we spent together and a shared dream of starting a baking business, I continue that vision today in her honor.

Every treat is baked fresh with bold flavor, care, and heart — from rich brownies and soft cookies to specialty desserts made to be enjoyed and shared.

Local delivery is available throughout Atlanta and surrounding areas, with nationwide shipping offered.
